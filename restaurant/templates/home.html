Key Features

Table Management

Efficiently manage restaurant tables with real-time status tracking. Available, Occupied, Bill Requested, and Closed statuses.

Order Management

Create, track, and manage orders from tables. Support for multiple items per order with automatic status transitions.

Billing System

Generate bills, calculate taxes, and process payments. Automatic table reset after payment.

Role-Based Access

Separate interfaces for Waiter, Cashier, and Manager with role-specific permissions and features.

Analytics & Reports

Real-time revenue tracking, bill analytics, and table usage reports for data-driven decisions.

Live Dashboard

Real-time visibility of all tables, orders, and revenue with auto-refreshing KPI cards and metrics.
